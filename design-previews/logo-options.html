A — seventeen dots · "seventeen."

A haiku tallied: 17 dots in rows of 5, 7, 5 — the seven in vermilion. Reads as poetry structure to those who know, as a quiet pattern to those who don't. Dots can fill in one-per-syllable as a progress meter while you write. Currently live in the homepage preview.
