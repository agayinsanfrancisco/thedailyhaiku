June 11, 1982 — E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial premieres in American theaters

Steven Spielberg's story of a stranded alien and the boy who hides him opened on 1,103 screens and never really left. Audiences came back week after week for an entire year; it overtook Star Wars to become the highest-grossing film ever and held the record for a decade. The famous silhouette — a bicycle lifting off across a full moon — became shorthand for wonder itself, and for the feeling that home is something you carry with you.
